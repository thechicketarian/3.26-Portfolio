About Me

Buenas, my name is Bexi Joibel! I am a Web Designer originally from Panama currently living in Austin, Texas. I love to work in all aspects of design but ultimately obsess over web design, UI/UX, and book design.

As a designer, I love to learn and evolve as I adapt my knowledge to ever evolving industry, whilst establishing my own style. I'm looking forward to joining a team with great opportunities to help enhance my skills, develop my potential, and meet higher expectations. When I am not designing, I love to play sports (Glory glory Man Uniteddddd!), draw, travel, and get lost in the world of YouTube!
